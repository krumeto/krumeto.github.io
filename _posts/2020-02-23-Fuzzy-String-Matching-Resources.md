## Fuzzy string matching in a corporate world

I've seen reasonable business projects blocked for months for a simple reason - same contract, customer, account has different names in different workflows and linking is difficult. Here comes fuzzy string matching.

My initial efforts arround the topic included playing around with the [FuzzyWuzzy](https://github.com/seatgeek/fuzzywuzzy). 
Here are a couple of other resources on the topic:

1. [Practical Business Python'post on record linking](https://pbpython.com/record-linking.html)
2. https://bergvca.github.io/2020/01/02/string-grouper.html)
3. https://bergvca.github.io/2017/10/14/super-fast-string-matching.html
4. http://blog.christianperone.com/2013/09/machine-learning-cosine-similarity-for-vector-space-models-part-iii/
5. https://recordlinkage.readthedocs.io/en/latest/about.html

For now, this is just a resource dump, but I plan to extend it to a blog post.
